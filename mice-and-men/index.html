Your Story Begins

The bus dropped you off miles from the ranch. Walking through the dusty California landscape, you think about why you're here — the dream of a better life, a piece of land, freedom from wandering.


When you reach the ranch, you meet the workers: George and his large, simple-minded companion Lennie, Curley the boss's aggressive son, his lonely wife, the stable buck Crooks, and many more.


The dream of owning your own land burns bright. Will you help George and Lennie achieve their dream?
